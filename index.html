I am currently a PhD. candidate in the Eco-evolutionary Dynamics Lab at McGill university. I completed my MSc under the supervision of Dr. Andrew Hendry at McGill University and Dr. Alison Derry at Université du Québec à Montréal. I am generally interested in how ecology and evolution interact to shape the diversity of species and ecosystems. More specifically, I am interested in how human disturbances affect contemporary populations in nature. I am also interested in how species adapt to environmental changes and whether these changes evolve in parallel across systems.
